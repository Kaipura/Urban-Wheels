About Us

Welcome to Urban Wheel! Cycling isn’t just a hobby for us it’s our way of life. Our shop was founded by a group of passionate cyclists who wanted to create a place where everyone, from beginners to seasoned pros, can find the perfect bike and all the gear they need to enjoy the ride. We pride ourselves on offering a wide range of high-quality bikes, including road bikes for speed enthusiasts, mountain bikes for adventure seekers, and electric bikes for those looking for a little extra boost. In addition to bikes, we carry a variety of accessories, clothing, and safety gear to ensure that every ride is comfortable and safe.

Our commitment goes beyond just selling bikes. We’re here to support our customers at every stage of their cycling journey. Our friendly and knowledgeable team is always ready to help you choose the right bike, find the best gear, or provide expert maintenance and repair services. We believe that a well-maintained bike is key to a great riding experience, and we’re here to make sure your bike is always in top condition. But Urban Wheel is more than just a bike shop. We’re a community of riders who share a love for cycling and the freedom it brings. Whether you’re commuting through the city, exploring new trails, or just enjoying a weekend ride, we’re here to support you every step of the way. When you choose Urban Wheel, you’re not just buying a bike you’re joining a community of like-minded people who are passionate about the open road.
