About Us

Founded in 2024, Urban Wheels started as a small, passionate team of cycling enthusiasts. Over the years, we have grown into a full-fledged online shop, dedicated to providing top-quality bikes and accessories to riders of all levels.

At Urban Wheels, we believe that cycling is more than just a hobby—it's a lifestyle. We’re committed to promoting the joy of cycling by offering products that enhance every ride. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to assist you in finding the perfect bike or gear.
